# mgm Template 2024 - Slide Templates Guide

> This document provides an overview of all slide templates in the mgm Technology Partners PowerPoint Template 2024.
> It serves as a reference for selecting the appropriate layouts for new presentations.

**Template file:** `mgm-Template-2024.pptx`
**Number of slides:** 46
**Format:** 16:9 (Widescreen)

## Design Elements

All slides consistently use:
- **mgm logo** top right (black on light, white on dark)
- **Color bar** bottom right (7 colors: Blue, Turquoise, Red, Orange/Yellow, Pink, Dark Blue, Green)
- **Claim** "Innovation Implemented." (only on title and closing slides)
- **Page info** vertical on the left: page number, date, confidentiality level
- **Color palette:** Gradient graphics in Orange/Yellow, Purple/Pink, Turquoise/Blue, Green/Turquoise

## Recommendations for Slide Selection

### Starting a Presentation
1. **Slide 1** as title slide
2. **Slide 6 or 7** as agenda (depending on scope)

### Structuring Chapters
- **Slide 8** (dark) or **Slide 45** (colored) as chapter divider
- **Slide 6/7** as interim agenda with highlight of the current chapter

### Presenting Content
- **Slide 5** for pure text content
- **Slide 10** for text with design accent
- **Slides 11-16** for text + image combinations (various colors and splits)
- **Slide 17** for quotes/testimonials

### Data & Numbers
- **Slide 9/20** for tables
- **Slide 43** for large key figures/KPIs
- **Slide 44** for diagrams/charts

### Processes & Steps
- **Slides 21/22** for 3 or 5 steps (cards)
- **Slide 23** for 6 elements (grid)
- **Slide 26** for stages/maturity levels
- **Slides 29/30** for ascending processes
- **Slide 34** for linear process chains
- **Slide 36** for 7-step workflows

### Structure & Relationships
- **Slide 24** for 4 quadrants (donut)
- **Slide 25** for 9 segments (wheel)
- **Slide 28** for hierarchies
- **Slide 31** for distributions
- **Slide 32** for cycles
- **Slide 33** for user journeys

### Project Management
- **Slide 35** for project goals
- **Slides 37/38** for team overviews
- **Slide 39** for milestone timelines
- **Slides 40-42** for Gantt charts/project plans

### Ending a Presentation
- **Slide 3** as closing/contact slide
